Visit Ivano-Frankivsk

Enjoy culture and vibe in this beautiful city in the west of Ukraine

Top three places to visit in the city

Attend an old Ratusha

Ratusha is a Town Hall and it has observation deck with the amazing view

Go for a piknic in the local park

Taras Shevchenko Park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out

Have a coffee on Stometrivka

Stometrivka is the main street where you can have coffee, listen to some street music bands etc
